❤️ Understanding Heart Failure

📌 What is Heart Failure?

Heart failure does not mean the heart has stopped. It means the heart is not pumping blood as efficiently as it should. This causes fatigue, swelling, shortness of breath, and fluid buildup in the body.

🧠 Why Does It Happen?

  • 💢 High blood pressure (most common cause)
  • 🫀 Coronary artery disease
  • 🩸 Diabetes
  • 🫁 Lung diseases
  • 🍔 Poor diet & unhealthy habits

🩺 How Is It Diagnosed?

  • 📊 Blood tests
  • 🫀 ECG
  • 🖥 Echocardiogram (EF%)
  • 🩺 Physical examination

✨ Can It Be Managed?

Yes! With proper diet, exercise, lifestyle changes, and medical monitoring, heart failure can be controlled effectively.
