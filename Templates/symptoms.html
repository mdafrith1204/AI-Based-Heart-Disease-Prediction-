💔 Symptoms of Heart Failure

🔍 Common Symptoms

  • 😮‍💨 Shortness of breath (especially during activity or while lying down)
  • 🦵 Swelling in legs, ankles, and feet
  • 😴 Extreme tiredness or fatigue
  • ❤️ Rapid or irregular heartbeat
  • 🥵 Persistent cough or wheezing (with white/pink mucus)
  • 😔 Difficulty concentrating or confusion
  • 🍽 Loss of appetite or nausea
  • ⚖️ Sudden weight gain due to fluid retention

⚠️ Emergency Symptoms

  • 💥 Chest pain
  • 😵 Severe shortness of breath
  • 😱 Fainting or dizziness
  • 🫁 Frothy cough with blood

If any emergency symptoms appear, seek immediate medical help.
